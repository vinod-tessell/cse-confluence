TAM Load Scoring Formula
🚨 P0/P1 Account
+8 pts per account with active P0/P1 Rationale: active fire — daily calls, escalations, executive pressure
⚠️ At Risk Account
+4 pts per At Risk account (no P0) Rationale: high watch load — imminent escalation risk, needs close monitoring
👀 Needs Attention Account
+2 pts per Needs Attention account Rationale: regular check-ins required, moderate monitoring overhead
🎫 Open SR Tickets
+0.5 pts each · capped at 15 pts Rationale: direct customer-facing workload across all accounts
⚙️ Open TS Eng Tickets
+0.3 pts each · capped at 8 pts Rationale: engineering coordination and follow-up overhead
📋 Accounts Managed
+3 pts per account · capped at 10 pts Rationale: base management overhead per account regardless of activity
🔄 Impl / Onboarding
+3 pts per active impl account Rationale: highest-touch phase — onboarding is effectively a second job
📊 Capacity Thresholds
≥ 85% → Over capacity 🔴 65–84% → Busy 🟠 35–64% → Available 🟡 < 35% → Has bandwidth 🟢 Soft cap: raw score 80 = 100%
⚙️ Raw score = sum of all factors above · divided by soft cap (80) · clamped to 100%  ·  Recommendation = least-loaded TAM under 70%
